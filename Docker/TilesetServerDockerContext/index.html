The datasets exposed below represent the buildings located in inner Lyon and regrouped in tiles. Those tilesets use the 3d-tiles standard format and are available separatly for three different time snapshots:

  • 2009,
  • 2012,
  • 2015.

These tilesets are derived from Lyon Métrople Open Data and the description of the full process used for their derivation production is publicly available (and uses only freely available software).

A simple 3D visualization tool allows an online vizualisation of the respective tilesets (use the slider at top of screen to switch between different time tags).
